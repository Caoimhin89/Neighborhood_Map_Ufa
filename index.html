About Ufa

Ufa is the capital of Russia's Bashkortostan Republic. Located in the southern Urals, it is right on the border of geographical Europe and Asia. The city has a population of approximately 1.5 million, divided roughly equally between ethnic Russians, Bashkirs, and Tatars with Orthodox Christianity and Sunni Islam being the two major religions. Russian and Bashkir are the two official languages of the Bashkortostan Republic, although Tatar is also widely spoken, especially in the northern part of the region. Bashkortostan is well-known for its agriculture, abundant minerals, booming energy sector, and its many famous writers and mathematicians. Ufa recently made world headlines as the host city for the 2015 BRICS economic summit.
